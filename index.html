About Me

I am a statistician and data scientist with 8+ years of experience developing and executing analyses, methods, and products supporting healthcare decision-making and policy.

My research focuses on Bayesian methods, hierarchical models, probabilistic simulations, and real-world evidence (RWD) to quantify uncertainty, model treatment outcomes, and optimize study designs.

I lead analytic teams at Mathematica, Inc., mentoring and translating complex findings into actionable insights to drive healthcare strategy and demonstrate clinical value.
