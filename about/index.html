About Jennifer

I am a PhD candidate at The University of Texas at Austin in the Department of Statistics and Data Sciences, working with professors James Scott, Jared Murray, Carlos Carvalho, and Abigail Aiken.

My research interests are in developing flexible Bayesian models for structured data, tree-based methods, and causal inference, with applications to public health and medicine. My recent work has focused on women’s healthcare, obstetrics, and family planning. Prior to joining UT, I completed my MS in Statistics at Texas A&M University.

I am also a researcher on Project SANA.
